Our Mission


Our mission is to offer a safe home for pets in need. While a pet lover may be looking to add a new member to their family, one may be looking for a ew pet owner to take over the care of their pet. In the past 10 years, Paw PetShop matched 10,000 pets with fun-loving owners and is planning to increase that number!

What We Offer


For pet lovers looking to adopt, we offer you the chance to get matched with your dream pet, the tools and guidelines to create a safe home for your dog or cat. If you're looking to put your pet up for adoption, our website will faciliate the process to make sure your pet ends up in good hands.
